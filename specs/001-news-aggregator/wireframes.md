- Clean card-based layout (modern news site style)
- **Search bar** at top - full-width, searches title field
- **Category filter** - Horizontal tabs for quick access to main categories
- **Sub-category dropdown** - Dynamically loads based on selected category
- **Date range filter** - Preset options (Today, Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Custom range)
- **Active filters** shown with count ("Showing 45 articles")
- **Clear Filters** button to reset all filters
- Main image on left (400x300px), metadata/content on right
- Category badge + Source (å¨›æ¨‚ Â· HK01)
- Author + relative time (2 hours ago, 1 day ago)
- 2-3 line excerpt (truncated)
- Hover effect: subtle shadow elevation
- Initial load: 10 articles
- Infinite scroll: Load 10 more when reaching bottom
- Mobile: Stack vertically (image on top, content below), filters collapse into drawer
